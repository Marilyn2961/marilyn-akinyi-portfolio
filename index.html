About me

The analyst who can write.
The writer who can analyse.

I grew up fascinated by two things: how language works and what patterns reveal. My linguistics degree gave me the first. My data science training at Moringa School gave me the second. My career at TESPOK is where I get to use both.

At TESPOK — Kenya's association for technology service providers — I manage communications, shape brand narratives, and make sure our work on Internet governance and ICT infrastructure is understood by the people who need to act on it. Translating the technical into the human is what I do every day.

Outside of that, I've been building a body of data science work — sentiment analysis, churn prediction, risk modelling — that I'm genuinely proud of. The projects on this site aren't just academic exercises; they're the kind of analysis I want to do for real organizations.

"I believe the most powerful insight is worthless if it can't be communicated. That's the gap I live in."
