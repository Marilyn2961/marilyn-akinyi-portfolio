My story

The analyst who can write.
The writer who can analyse.

I grew up fascinated by two things: how language works and what patterns reveal. My linguistics degree gave me the first. My data science training at Moringa School gave me the second. My career at TESPOK is where I get to use both every day.

At TESPOK — Kenya's association for technology service providers — I manage communications, shape brand narratives, and make sure complex work on Internet governance and ICT infrastructure is understood by the people who need to act on it.

Alongside that, I've been building a body of data science work — sentiment analysis, churn prediction, risk modelling — that I'm genuinely proud of. The projects here aren't academic exercises. They're the kind of thinking I bring to every problem.

"The most powerful insight is worthless if it can't be communicated. That's the gap I live in."
