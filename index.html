About Me

I am a Brand and Communications professional with a background in Linguistics, Media, and Communication, and hands-on experience working in Internet governance, ICT, and multi-stakeholder environments. I currently work in Public Relations and Communications at Technology Service Providers of Kenya (TESPOK), where I support brand consistency, strategic messaging, and visual communication across events, reports, digital platforms, and stakeholder engagements.

I am particularly motivated by roles that use brand and design to advance public-interest technology, inclusion, and a trustworthy Internet.
